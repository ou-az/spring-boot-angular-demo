How to Choose the Right Mortgage

Selecting the right mortgage is an important decision that impacts your financial future. Consider these factors:

  • Your credit score and financial history
  • How long you plan to stay in the home
  • The amount of down payment you can afford
  • Whether you're a first-time homebuyer
  • Your eligibility for special programs (VA, FHA, etc.)
